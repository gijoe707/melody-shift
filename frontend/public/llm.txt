# Melody Shift - Spotify to YouTube Music Playlist Transfer

## Overview
Melody Shift is a free, open-source web application that allows users to seamlessly transfer their music playlists from Spotify to YouTube Music. The tool supports both individual playlist transfers and bulk migrations.

## Key Features
- **Free Playlist Transfer**: No subscription or payment required
- **Spotify to YouTube Music**: Direct playlist migration between platforms
- **Multiple Transfer Options**: 
  - Transfer via Spotify account login
  - Transfer via public playlist link
  - Private YouTube Music account support
  - Public YouTube Music playlist creation
- **Bulk Operations**: Transfer multiple playlists at once
- **Track Matching**: Intelligent song matching between platforms
- **Progress Tracking**: Real-time transfer status and results
- **Open Source**: Available on GitHub at https://github.com/OwaisSafa/melody-shift

## Use Cases
1. **Platform Migration**: Moving your entire music library from Spotify to YouTube Music
2. **Playlist Sharing**: Converting Spotify playlists to YouTube Music format
3. **Music Discovery**: Transferring curated playlists between services
4. **Backup**: Creating copies of playlists across platforms

## Technical Details
- **Frontend**: React, TypeScript, Vite, TailwindCSS
- **Backend**: Python, FastAPI, ytmusicapi
- **Authentication**: Supports both OAuth and header-based authentication
- **Privacy**: User headers are processed securely and not stored permanently

## Target Audience
- Spotify users switching to YouTube Music
- Music enthusiasts managing playlists across platforms
- Users wanting to backup their playlists
- People sharing music collections with friends on different platforms
